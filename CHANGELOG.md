# Changelog

## [v1.0.0] — July 2026 — Initial Release

### Features

- Four filing paths: ITR-1 (simple), ITR-2 domestic, ITR-2 foreign assets, ITR-2 high income
- ITR-1 step-by-step guide (15-minute filing)
- ITR-2 for foreign asset holders (eTrade, Vested, Equinix RSU/ESOP)
- Schedule FA, FSI, TR complete guidance
- Schedule AL with all asset categories for income > ₹50 lakhs
- Surcharge rates with worked example and marginal relief explanation
- New vs Old regime comparison with Form 10-IEA
- HRA calculation including WFH and rent-to-parents scenarios
- Salary arrears — Section 89 relief + Form 10E
- EPF taxation rules
- Multiple employer / job change handling
- Form 12BB declaration guide
- Zerodha P&L capital gains integration
- Form 1042-S reading guide
- DTAA rates reference
- Schedule 112A CSV guidance
- Self Assessment Tax payment steps
- Advance tax obligations for high earners
- Common portal errors and fixes
